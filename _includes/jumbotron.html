UPDATE!

In March 2020, the staff of BSides Rochester made the decision to initially postpone our 10th Anniversary event to sometime in the fall of 2020. However, given the rapid changes to the COVID-19 situation nationally and globally we have decided that the responsible thing is to postpone our event tentatively to March 2021.

This was a hard decision, but certainly not a difficult one. The health and safety of everyone involved with BSides Rochester is our top priority. Existing tickets will be honored for the new date. Contact us if you need a refund. Please watch our website http://bsidesroc.com and follow our social media channels for updated information as we have it available.
