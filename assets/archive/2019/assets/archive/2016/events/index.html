Events

Here’s a list of the events that will be going on during BSidesROC 2015

    • Presentations – industry professionals discuss their research & other security topics.  CPE credit is also possible for attending these – consult your certifying organization’s website for criteria.
    • Hacker Battleship – score points by breaking into computers and applications
    • GPG Intro and Key Signing Party – Learn how to use GPG and attend our beer laden Key Signing Party
    • Crypto Challenge – take a crack at breaking encrypted puzzles
    • TOOOL Lock Pick Village – learn how to pick locks using provided tools (run by TOOOL ROC).
    • Résumélee – Show off your mad self-doxing chops (wait… what?) and maybe get a gig. (Oh, just go read it)
    • NSF Workshop – An open conversation with security experts on network attack behaviors
