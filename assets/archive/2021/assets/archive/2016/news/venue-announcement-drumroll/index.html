Thursday January 14th, 2016

Venue Announcement… drumroll…

We wanted to officially announce the 2016 Security B-Sides Rochester event to be held Saturday, April 23rd 2016. Not only do we have a bunch of new ideas, games, presentations, and fun, we have a big announcement that makes this year different and a first for the BSidesROC team.

This year, our venue will be….

 

We’re very excited to try out a bigger venue for the first year ever. RIT will make it easier for students to attend while giving our repeat attendees a different venue to enjoy. Expect the same fun and hacking goodness that you have come to enjoy like in previous years but with better parking options! 🙂

How can you help?

  • Spread the word to your friends and coworkers and random people in your social network.
  • Help us find sponsors that you know so we can raise funds to make the con awesome.
  • You can always volunteer to get the real experience of what the conference will be like.

Get in touch with us on Twitter, email, or whatever encrypted chat protocol you prefer.

Registration for this year is not yet open so please save the date and check back soon. In the mean time, check out the CFP area to submit your ideas for talks.
