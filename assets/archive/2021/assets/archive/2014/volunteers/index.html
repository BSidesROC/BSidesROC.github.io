Volunteers

BSidesROC is a non-profit venture that relies on its dedicated pool of volunteers to make it run. We’re looking for people to help us setup, tear down, provide security, etc etc. If you’re available on the day of the con, and want to get in on all the behind-the-scenes fun, we could use your help. Please fill out the volunteer sign-up form and let us know what you want to do! Our volunteer spots are filled up. Thanks. Check back next year.
