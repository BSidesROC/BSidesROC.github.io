Venue

BSidesROC has a new location this year – the historic German House. We’ve documented some of the reasons for the change here, but what you need to know when you show up is that the conference will begin in the Zeppa Auditorium, which is the entrance closest to the street. When you arrive, go there first to get checked in and receive your badge.

Address

315 Gregory St
Rochester, NY

Parking and Transportation

The map above shows parking areas in green. This includes the German House Parking Lot as well as the church parking lot across the street. If all else fails, there is street parking available around the area. See the map for reference.

Hotel Information

Coming in from out of town and need a place to stay? Good news! We’ve worked with the Rochester Hyatt Regency to score a discounted rate of $120/night. If you’re interested in taking advantage of this, call Donna Marsh @ (585) 794 – 4829 to register.
Note: You must mention “BSides Rochester” to get the discounted rate.
