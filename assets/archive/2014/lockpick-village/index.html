Lockpick Village

Join one of the favorite parts of BSidesROC, the lock pick village. The Open Organization Of Lockpickers or (TOOOL) will be running a booth to teach people how to pick locks, give an introduction to physical security, and share the legality and ethics of the fun hobby.

You can expect:

  • Introduction to lock picking using a training lock set
  • Presentations related to picking different styles of locks
  • Learn about physical security
  • Lock pick sets for sale
