Sponsors

 

Secure Network Technologies

Secure Network Technologies, Inc. is a full service information security firm dedicated to providing superior security services and delivering actionable, real-world results. Secure Network staff understand what it takes to build a security program that fits the unique needs of your business.

 

Syncurity Networks

Syncurity Networks, LLC offers computer security training, network security monitoring and consulting (NSM and SIEM), penetration testing and security operations management consulting and services to local and national companies. Syncurity can also help you create a security program to help meet internal or compliance requirements.

Grey Castle

GreyCastle Security, is an information security consulting firm, focusing on risk management, awareness and operational security. Our company was established because a need existed for an information security consulting firm truly focused on managing risks in people, process and technology, not pushing the latest hardware and software.

 

 

Rapid7

Rapid7 offers simple, innovative software solutions that give you visibility into the risk associated with your IT, your users and the real threats you face. Their software helps you quickly prioritize threats, manage risk, and take the right steps to improve your organization’s security.

 

Intrepidus Group

Intrepidus Group is a leading provider of mobile application and device security services. We assess iPhone, Android, and Blackberry, applications, train developers to code defensively for these platforms, and evaluate the security mechanisms of cutting edge telecommunications products and smart phone devices.

 

Positron Security

Positron Security can help you at any and all levels of the security process to produce a minimal-risk system. If you are buying a new server, writing a new website, or just want your existing infrastructure audited, they can fulfill your security needs.

Siege Technologies

 

 

 

 

Siege Technologies is focused on advancing the state of the art in advanced computer security and information warfare technologies.

Our discriminating strengths are our commitment to hiring uniquely qualified personnel, understanding of the problem space and customer community, and the development of innovative solutions to complex problems. Siege staff have extensive demonstrated expertise in software/host/network red teaming, protocol analysis, protection techniques, security engineering, and R&D for various DoD, intelligence and commercial organizations.

Juniper Networks

Juniper Networks’ products and technologies run the world’s largest and most demanding networks today, enabling their customers to create value and accelerate business success within the new, rapidly changing global marketplace. Their customers include the top 130 global service providers, the Fortune Global 100, as well as hundreds of federal, state and local government agencies and higher education organizations throughout the world.
